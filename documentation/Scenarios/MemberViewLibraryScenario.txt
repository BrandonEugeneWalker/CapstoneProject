2.	A member login to website.
3.	A member clicks to view media page.
4.	A member view media library.
5.	A member logs out.

	.4: A member not view media page without logging in.
	.5: A member re-attempts to login with correct information.
	.6: A member logs in.
	.7: A member views media library.
	.8: A member logs out.
