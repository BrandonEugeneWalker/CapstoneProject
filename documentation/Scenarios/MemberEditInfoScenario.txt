Member Edit Information:
1.	A member loads library website.
2.	A member logs in to website.
3.	A member clicks profile page.
4.	A member clicks edit information.
5.	A member enters information.
6.	A member clicks save.
7.	A member has editted their information.
8.	A member logs out.
Alternate:
2a: Member no log in) The profile button only appears when logged in.
5a: Member enters invalid data) A member inputs invalid password that does not contain a upper letter, a lower letter, a digit, and a symbol, or is not of length 6.
	.6: A member is informed that data is invalid.
	.7: A member re-attempt to save with valid data.
